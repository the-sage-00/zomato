Our Solution

KitchenPulse: 5 Signals > 1 Button

🏆 Corrected Dwell Time

GPS-based rider wait time, decomposed to extract pure kitchen wait. Passive, behavioral, zero-fraud signal.

+ Smart FOR Validation

Score each merchant's FOR reliability 0-100. Trust or ignore automatically.

📱 Merchant Behavior

Order ack latency, app interaction patterns during rush → proxy for kitchen load.

+ AKAI (Ambient Kitchen AI)

On-device TFLite model senses kitchen rush via ambient sound. Leading indicator — catches rush BEFORE rider is dispatched.

🌍 External Busyness

Google Popular Times — free crowdsourced foot traffic. 30-min lag, used as context not primary.
