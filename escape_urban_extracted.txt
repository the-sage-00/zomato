Despite massive spending, Delhi‚Äôs waste management remains fundamentally

1. Manual Reporting:

2. Blind-Spot Routing: Garbage collection trucks follow fixed, pre-defined

Route ineÔ¨Äiciency is estimated at 30-40%.

Anti-Spam Defense (3 Layers)
1. AI Vision Validation: YOLOv8 checks if the submitted photo actually
contains waste. Non-waste images are auto-rejected.
2. Live GPS Enforcement: GPS timestamp must be live. Downloaded or
old photos are detected and flagged.
3. Community Cross-Verification: Reports verified by 2+ independent
citizens receive a credibility boost. Repeated fake reporters face token
penalties and cooldowns.
